Dark And Stormy

Ingredients

  • 3 oz. ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 2 oz. dark rum (we used Gosling's)
  • Candied ginger, for serving

Description:

  1. To a glass with ice, add ginger beer and lime juice, then top with dark rum and garnish with candied ginger.
