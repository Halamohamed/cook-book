Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-large boneless skinless chicken breasts OR 4-6 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • juice of 1 medium lemon (about 2 tablespoons)
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup kalamata olives
  • 1 cup roughly chopped roasted red peppers
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • fresh thyme,', 'oregano,', 'or basil for garnish (optional)

Description:

  • Season chicken with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Cook in a large skillet over medium-high heat 1-2 minutes on each side til browned.
  • Transfer to greased slow cooker.
  • Add onions, olives, red peppers, and capers to slow cooker (tuck them around the sides so they aren't covering up the chicken).
  • Whisk together italian seasoning, lemon juice, and garlic and pour over chicken.
  • Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or on high for 2 hours.
  • Garnish with fresh thyme, oregano, or basil and serve.
