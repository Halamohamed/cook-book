Classic Sidecar

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. Cognac
  • 2 tbsp. Cointreau
  • 1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice

Description:

  1. In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, shake Cognac, Cointreau, and lemon juice until cold. Strain into a coupe.
