Raspberry Daiquiri

Ingredients

  • 1 oz. white rum
  • 1 oz. lime juice plus 1 lime slice, for serving
  • 1 oz. raspberry-lime syrup

Directions

  1. In a cocktail shaker filled with ice, shake rum, lime juice, and raspberry-lime syrup until cold.
  2. Strain into a coup and serve with a slice of lime.
